ABOUT

My name is Uyen Lawless, and I'm currently a junior at Eastern Washington University pursuing a Bachelor's of Design in Visual Communication Design, along with a User Experience Design Certificate. I'm originally from Vietnam, and have arrived in Spokane, Washington since June, 2015. I've stayed in Spokane since and gotten married to my other half in 2018.
I really enjoy every aspects of design that I've learned so far, anything from typography, logo design, user experience design to digital photography, etc. I have been and will always be curious and fascinated about design every day in my life.
When I'm not designing, I enjoy watching horror, thriller or mystery shows or movies. Watching tons and tons of horror movies before coming to the U.S. was how I got to learn English back then. I also love playing chords while singing with my digital piano, and hanging out with my family.
A huge motivation that has got me to start designing is alpacas. I know, they're just awesome!
